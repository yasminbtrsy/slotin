*2. Project Title*
'SlotIn - sport Court Booking System'

*3. Introduction*
- Problem statement
Many sport facilities users face difficulties when tryinh to book courts due to the absence of centralized digital platform. Common issues include double bookings, no real time availability information and the reliance on manual booking methods like phone calls or walk-in booking.This affects both users who want reliable booking experience and facility owners who struggle to manage reservation efficiently.

- Motivation
A mobile based booking system provides a convenient and efficient solution for managing sport court reservation with the increasing use of smartphones. SlotIn aims to simplify the entire booking process for both the general public and facility administrators.

- Relevance of the proposed app
SlotIn addresses a problem faced by sport enthusiasts by digitizing the booking process. It eliminates scheduling conflicts by delivering a better experience for users and court owners.

*4. Objectives*
1. To develop a mobile application that allows users to browse and book sport courts in real time
2. To enable admins to manage court listings, availability and incoming bookings efficiently
3. To provide automated push notifications for booking confirmations and reminders
4. Ti integrate Firebase services for secure authentication and real-time data storage

*5. Target Users*
Primary - General public, sport enthusiasts who want to check availability and make bookings conveniently via mobile
Secondary - Court owner, individuals that responsible for managing courts, handling bookings and monitoring facility usage 

*6. Features & Functionalities*
Auth & Profile: Register, login/logout, role-based access
Booking & scheduling: Browse for courts by date and select time slot, confirm booking, view booking details
Dashboard/Analysis: view and manage all bookings (owner). view upcoming and past bookings
Court management:view court details and availability
